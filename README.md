# 家庭用电节能分析系统 | Household Energy Saving Analysis System
## ICC Global Invention Convention Entry Project

## 1. 项目简介 | Project Introduction
### 中文
本项目为ICC全球发明大会参赛作品，基于Python控制台开发的家庭用电节能分析系统。可实现电费与碳排放核算、多日用电数据统计分析、家电能耗管理、峰谷电价测算、五维度用电习惯智能评分，为家庭提供专业化节电方案。
项目核心算法与业务逻辑由主创独立设计编码，可视化界面模块为工程化辅助开发；项目已于2026年5月完成区块链+国家授时中心可信时间戳原创确权，存证凭证受北京互联网法院司法认可。

### English
This work is an entry for the ICC Global Invention Convention, a household energy-saving analysis system developed based on Python console. It supports electricity bill & carbon emission calculation, multi-day power consumption statistical analysis, household appliance energy management, peak & valley electricity price estimation, and 5-dimensional intelligent scoring of electricity usage habits, providing professional power-saving solutions for families.
All core algorithms and business logic are independently designed and coded by the authors, while the visual interface modules are auxiliary engineering developments. The project finished copyright confirmation via blockchain and trusted timestamp issued by the National Time Service Center in May 2026, and the certification is legally recognized by the Beijing Internet Court.

## 2. 核心功能 | Core Functions
### 中文
1. 月度电费、总用电量与碳排放自动核算
2. 多日用电数据录入、统计及用电波动可视化分析
3. 家用电器功率信息的增、删、改、查管理
4. 峰谷分时电费自动计算，对比常规电价测算节约成本
5. 五大维度用电行为评级，生成个性化节能优化建议

### English
1. Automatic calculation of monthly electricity bill, total power consumption and carbon emissions
2. Multi-day power data entry, statistics and visual analysis of electricity fluctuation
3. Add, delete, modify and query management for household electrical appliance power parameters
4. Automatic peak-valley electricity cost calculation to estimate saved expenses compared with standard electricity price
5. Electricity behavior rating from five dimensions to generate personalized energy-saving suggestions
